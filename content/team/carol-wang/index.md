## Carol Wang

**Role:** Research Scientist — Multimodal AI

**Focus Areas:** Vision-Language Models, Document Understanding, Visual Reasoning

### Bio

Carol is a research scientist specializing in multimodal AI systems. Her work bridges computer vision and natural language processing, enabling AI agents to understand and reason over visual information. She received her Ph.D. from Peking University and has published extensively at CVPR, ECCV, and AAAI.

### Selected Publications

- "Cross-Modal Reasoning for Software UI Understanding" — CVPR 2025
- "Document-Aware Code Generation from Screenshots" — ECCV 2024
- "Visual Chain-of-Thought Prompting" — AAAI 2025
